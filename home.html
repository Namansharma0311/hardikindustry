About

Traditional craft.
Modern manufacturing.

Hardik Industries has been producing premium buffing material for the polishing trade since 1980 — four decades of refining what a consistent, high-performing buff should deliver under demanding conditions.

Our buffs finish utensils, cutlery, kitchenware, and decorative metalware in workshops across India. We pair traditional craftsmanship with controlled, modern production so every buff leaving our factory performs with the same consistency.
